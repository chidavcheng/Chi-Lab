#### Teaching Philosophy

My teaching is guided by three principles: **active learning** through hands-on experiments and real data; **adaptability** by continuously refining courses based on student feedback; and **accessibility** by making complex concepts approachable for all students through clear explanations and a supportive environment.

These principles are reflected in my completion of UNSW's <a href="https://www.unsw.edu.au/teaching/professional-development/course-design-institute" target="_blank">Course Design Institute (CDI)</a> and the <a href="https://tll.mit.edu/programming/grad-student-programming/kaufman-teaching-certificate-program/" target="_blank">Kaufman Teaching Certificate Program (KTCP)</a> at MIT.
