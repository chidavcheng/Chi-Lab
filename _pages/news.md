## 2025

**Nov 2025** — New paper published! *Edge-activated graphene nanopores for thermally robust hydrogen membrane separations* in **Nature Communications**. Congratulations to all co-authors! 🎉

**Nov 2025** — David was invited as a panelist to speak at the Net-ZERO-Next-Gen NSW Assembly, Sydney, Australia.

**Oct 2025** — Chi Lab awarded **AU$663k** from the Australian Research Council Discovery Project for *"Nanofluidic modulation for precision molecular separations."* 🎉

**Oct 2025** — Chi Lab awarded **AU$200k** from the UNSW–Chinese Academy of Sciences Collaborative Research Program Stage II for *"Nanofluidic membrane electrode assemblies for highly efficient green hydrogen production."*

**Sep 2025** — Hongzhe Chen joins the Chi Lab as a PhD student. Welcome aboard! 🎉

**Jul 2025** — David gave an invited talk at the Membrane Society of Australasia (MSA) Industry Seminar, Wollongong, Australia.

**Jun 2025** — Feifan Yang from the University of British Columbia, Canada, joins the Chi Lab as a PhD student. Welcome aboard! 🎉

**Feb 2025** — Jiahao Ding, Xuanming Chang, and Yihua Song join the Chi Lab as MPhil students. Welcome! 🎉
